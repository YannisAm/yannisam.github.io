Welcome to Erifyli Export

At Erifyli Exports, our roots run deep — shaped by years of firsthand experience in the journey of fruit, from soil to shipment. With over a decade immersed in the rhythms of nature and the pursuit of quality, we’ve set out to share the freshest produce with the world. Our mission is simple: to deliver premium fruit with integrity, consistency, and care. We aim to build lasting relationships through transparency, reliability, and a love for what we do. From farm to shelf, we work to ensure that every shipment reflects our high standards — and our promise to grow sustainably, trade fairly, and exceed expectations.
